Install BJAssist in Chrome

1

Download the extension

⬇ Download BJAssist (.zip, ~120 KB)

Chrome may ask you to confirm the download — click Keep.

2

Unzip it somewhere permanent

Windows: right-click the downloaded file → Extract All…
Mac: double-click the file.

You'll get a folder called bjassist-extension. Move it somewhere it can stay — e.g. your Documents folder. Chrome loads the extension from this folder, so if you delete it later, the extension stops working.

3

Open Chrome's extensions page & turn on Developer mode

Type chrome://extensions into Chrome's address bar and press Enter. (Edge: edge://extensions · Brave: brave://extensions)

Flip the Developer mode switch in the top-right corner of that page. Three new buttons appear.

4

Click "Load unpacked" and pick the folder

Click the Load unpacked button (top-left), then select the bjassist-extension folder you unzipped in step 2 and click Select Folder.

BJAssist appears in your extension list immediately — that's it installed.

5

Pin it and play

Click the puzzle-piece icon 🧩 to the right of Chrome's address bar and hit the 📌 pin next to BJAssist, so the B button is always visible.

Now open your casino and sit at a blackjack or video poker table — the advice badge appears over the game automatically as you're dealt in. Your first hands are free.

Good to know

  • "Developer mode" is just Chrome's name for loading extensions from a folder instead of the Web Store. Chrome may show a small reminder about it when it starts — that's normal, click × to dismiss.
  • Updating: when we ship a new version, download the new zip, replace the folder's contents, then click the ↻ refresh arrow on the BJAssist card in chrome://extensions.
  • Already paid? Click the B icon and paste your license key in the popup — or use the activation page.
